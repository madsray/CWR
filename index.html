Our team makes us who we are. Pretty amazing. We are tied together with a wealth of experience and fresh perspectives. Mike Ray
CPMR | President

Since 1995... Mike started his career with The Wingard Corporation in 1986 covering the Houston area. He completed his formal education at Texas A&M University receiving a Bachelor of Science degree in industrial distribution. Mike served thirteen years as a Captain in the Texas Army National Guard. He became a principal at Corbett Wingard & Ray Inc. in 1995. Mike has won multiple awards and utilizes his military background, plumbing wholesaler experience and over thirty years of plumbing sales experience to lead one of the top-rated sales agencies in Texas.
