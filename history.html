Where We Began

1946: Lester Wingard founds premier manufacturer representative agency, The Wingard Corporation partnering with General Wire Spring Company & Bemis – factories we continue to represent to this day 1995: Noble Corbett & Mike Ray purchase The Wingard Corporation, establishing Corbett, Wingard & Ray 2009: Mike Ray becomes sole proprietor after purchasing Noble Corbett’s share in the company The team at Corbett, Wingard & Ray has a combined experience level totaling more than 200 years in the plumbing industry. Corbett, Wingard & Ray’s cornerstone is built around continuous technical and product expertise. We pride ourselves on staying sharp with professional training. Internally our training goals include having all our sales representatives either certified in CSP or CPMR.
